Agreement for Travel Services

  1. Booking & Reservations: All bookings made through Hashir Travel are subject to availability. A booking is only confirmed once the initial deposit or full payment is received and a confirmation voucher is issued.
  2. Pricing & Payments: All prices are quoted in USD/Local Currency as per current exchange rates. Hashir Travel reserves the right to adjust pricing in the event of significant fuel surcharges or government-imposed tax changes.
  3. Cancellation & Refunds: Cancellation policies vary by airline, hotel, and tour operator.
    • Standard Tours: Cancellations made 30 days prior are eligible for a 75% refund.
    • Non-Refundable Services: Certain flight tickets and promotional hotel stays are non-refundable.
  4. Visa Services: While Hashir Travel provides expert consultancy and document processing, the final decision to grant a visa rests solely with the respective Embassy or Consulate. Service fees are non-refundable regardless of the visa outcome.
  5. Travel Insurance: We strongly recommend that all travelers obtain comprehensive travel insurance. Hashir Travel is not liable for health issues, accidents, or loss of personal property during the journey.

Your Data Security & Privacy

  1. Data Collection: We collect personal information (Name, Passport Details, Email, and Contact Number) solely for the purpose of booking travel services and complying with international aviation and immigration laws.
  2. Data Usage: Your information is shared only with third-party providers (Airlines, Hotels, and Insurance Companies) necessary to complete your travel arrangements. We do not sell your data to marketing agencies.
  3. Payment Security: All online transactions are processed via secure, encrypted gateways. Hashir Travel does not store credit card or bank login details on our local servers.
  4. Cookies: Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and remember your destination preferences for future visits.
  5. User Rights: You have the right to request a copy of your data or ask for its deletion from our records at any time by contacting privacy@hashirtravel.com.
